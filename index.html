About Us

Welcome to SmartFoodie GmbH, where we focus on innovative and sustainable food solutions. Our mission is to provide convenient and healthy eating options for students, working professionals, and those who don't have enough time to eat. Our company SmartFoodie GmbH specializes in innovative catering solutions. With the help of our fully automatic food vending machines equipped with a steamer, we can offer our customers freshly prepared food in just 2-3 minutes. In today's fast-paced world, we recognize the importance of convenient meals and with the SmartFoodie vending machines we guarantee that speed and convenience do not come at the expense of taste or nutritional quality. The majority of our meals are made here in Munich, which allows us to guarantee quality and support the local economy. At SmartFoodie, we offer more than just food; we offer a time-saving, freshly cooked, nutritious and hot meal that is always ready when you are.
